Welcome to Surat City

The diamond hub of India, reowned for its vibrant textile industry and rich culture heritage. Discover the 5 famous places in Surat City

1. Surat Castle

Explore the Surat Castle, a masjestic 16th century fortress standing as a testamnet to the city's resilient history and architectural grandeur.

2. Dumas Beach

Stroll along Duman beach, a serene coastal excape famed for its black sand and captivating sunrises.

3. Sardar patel museum

Visit the Sadar Patel Museum in Surat, wher history and art converge to celebrate India's rich heritage.

4. Dutch Garden

Wander through the Dutch Garden in Surat, an oasis of tranquility adorned with colonial-era tomobs and lush greenery.

5. Snow Park Family Entertainment Center

Enjoy a frosty adventrue at Snow Park Family Entertainment in Surat, where winter fun awaits with exciting snow activites and attractions.
